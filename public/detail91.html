Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:
  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken (marinated in yogurt and spices)
  • Onions (fried until golden brown)
  • Tomatoes (chopped)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom)
  • Biryani masala
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves (chopped)
  • Saffron-infused milk
  • Ghee or oil
  • Raita (for serving)
Description:

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful dish made by layering marinated chicken with long-grain basmati rice, aromatic spices, and fried onions. The chicken is cooked to tender perfection, while the rice absorbs the rich flavors of the spices, creating a delightful combination of taste and texture. Topped with saffron-infused milk, fresh coriander, and mint leaves, Chicken Biryani is an indulgent dish that is perfect for special occasions or a hearty family meal. Serve with cooling raita for a balanced and satisfying meal experience!
